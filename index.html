About Us

Your Pathway to

Global Education

Founded in 2019, WI Education Consultancy Lalitpur Pvt. Ltd. is driven by passionate industry experts dedicated to guiding students toward a successful international education journey. We provide reliable, first-hand insights into student life abroad, balancing studies with part-time work, and seizing opportunities along the way.

Expert Guidance – Get personalized support from experienced professionals.

Reliable Information – First-hand insights into international student life.
